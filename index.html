Short Bio

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Xidian University (XDU), supervised by Prof. Xiangrong Zhang. My research interests include weakly supervised learning, object detection, and remote sensing image analysis.

Recently, I'm working on weakly supervised object detection (WSOD), weakly supervised semantic segmentation (WSSS), remote sensing object detection (RSOD), and hyperspectral image change detection (HICD).
